# Brand Kit Skill

Generate a complete brand visual identity system.

## Inputs
- brand_name: Name of the brand
- industry: Industry or niche
- personality: 3-5 adjectives (bold, elegant, modern, etc.)
- color_preference: Optional color direction

## Output
- Logo concept A (minimalist wordmark)
- Logo concept B (icon + wordmark)
- Moodboard with 5-color palette
- Pattern/texture asset
- Color palette recommendation (5 hex codes)
- Typography pairing (2 Google Fonts)
