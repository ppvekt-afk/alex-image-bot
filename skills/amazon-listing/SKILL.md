# Amazon Product Listing Pack

Generate 4 Amazon-optimized product images: hero (white background), lifestyle, infographic with features, and detail closeup.

## Inputs
- product_name: Name of the product
- product_category: Amazon category
- key_features: Comma-separated top features
- target_buyer: Who buys this (optional)

## Output
- Hero image (pure white background)
- Lifestyle/context shot
- Feature infographic with callouts
- Closeup detail shot
